Information

Flights

We understand that Perth is going to be a long flight for some and we appreciate the journey taken by everyone to celebrate with us. We recommend booking as far as possible in advance. We will be keeping a lookout for any specials.

Airport Transport

Getting to and from Perth Airport: The train runs from the Airport to Perth City then onwards. The trainline to the northern suburbs is on the freeway – it does not reach the coastal suburbs. Alternatively it's a 20-minute Uber/Taxi from the Airport into Perth City.

Things to see in Perth

We have added some of our favourite things to see and do around Perth to the map below. Click on the icons to expand for more information and ideas. There are many options close to the city and coast but don't forget to consider a drive South to Margaret River if staying longer in WA.

Our favourite eats in Perth

If you're travelling up the northern coast, drop into Little Bay Bar & Eatery, Mister D'arcy and Lot One Kitchen. If you're heading south to Freo, our favourite spots for a drink and meal are Gage Roads Brewery, Little Creatures, Running with Thieves and Nieuw Ruin, topped off with Swan River Gelato.

Accomodation

We recommend booking accommodation as far ahead as possible; however, some locations do not release rooms until 6 months prior so it can be worth calling. Depending on your preference for exploring WA our accommodation recommendations are: Perth City, coastal hubs such as Hillarys and Sorrento, Scarborough, or the Swan Valley.

Q & A
Should I bring a gift?

Our friends and family taking the time to celebrate and support us is all we ask for, so please just bring yourself.

Can I bring my kiddo?

We politely request this be an adult occasion (infants in arms are welcome).

What should I wear?

The dress code is cocktail/semi-formal but it's most important you bring your dancing shoes!

What's the West Coast weather like?

Perth in March is still quite warm. As the ceremony is later in the day, we're expecting it to have cooled off a little - the Ceremony is planned indoors, with the Reception and dancing under the stars.

Any final tips for my trip to Perth?

Unlike NSW, VIC and SA, WA does not observe daylight savings so there will be a 2:30-3 hour time difference - you’ll lose time travelling back east. Also, some accommodation in Perth is only available to book 6 months out.
